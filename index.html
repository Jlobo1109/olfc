How to Reach Us

By Road: Our Lady of Fatima Church is located off the Ghodbunder Road at the Majiwada Junction, opposite the Municipal Office.

By Train: The nearest railway station is Thane Railway Station, approximately 5 km away.

By Bus: Several bus routes pass through Majiwada Junction, making it easily accessible by public transport.
