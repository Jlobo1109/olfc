Legion of Mary


The Legion of Mary is a lay apostolic association of Catholics who, with the sanction of the Church and under the powerful leadership of Mary Immaculate, serve the Church and their neighbor on a voluntary basis in about 170 countries. The group meets weekly for prayer, planning, and discussion in a family setting, and performs apostolic work in the parish community.

Altar Servers


The Altar Servers are a dedicated group of young parishioners who assist the priest during Mass and other liturgical services. They are trained in the rituals and responsibilities of the altar, helping to ensure that the liturgy is conducted with reverence and order. The group meets regularly for practice and spiritual formation.

Lectors Ministry


The Lectors Team is responsible for the preparation and smooth conduct of all liturgical celebrations in the parish. This includes organizing lectors, extraordinary ministers of Holy Communion, altar servers, and coordinating with the choir. The team ensures that every Mass and service is prayerful and meaningful for the congregation.

Music Ministry


The Music Ministry animates the liturgy by leading the congregation in song and providing instrumental accompaniment. The ministry includes choir members, musicians, and cantors who serve at Sunday Masses, feast days, and special parish events. Their goal is to foster active participation and a prayerful atmosphere through music.

Eucharistic Ministry


The Eucharistic Ministry assists in the distribution of Holy Communion during Mass, ensuring that the Body of Christ are shared reverently and efficiently with the congregation. Ministers also bring the Eucharist to the sick, elderly, and homebound who cannot attend Mass, extending Christ’s presence to them. Their mission is to serve with humility, respect, and devotion, helping the faithful experience the fullness of the Eucharistic celebration.

Ladies Sodality


The Ladies Sodality is a group of women parishioners dedicated to fostering devotion to Our Lady, supporting parish activities, and engaging in charitable works. Members meet regularly for prayer, spiritual formation, and to organize events that benefit the parish and the wider community.

Charismatic Prayer Group


The Charismatic Prayer Group is a vibrant community of believers who come together to experience the power of the Holy Spirit through prayer, praise, worship, and the Word of God. Guided by Scripture and open to the gifts of the Spirit, we seek a deeper personal relationship with Jesus Christ and strive to live out our faith with joy, love, and service. All are welcome to join us in prayer, song, and fellowship as we grow together in the Spirit every Wednesday after 7:00PM Mass.
