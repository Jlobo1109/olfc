Spiritual Ministries

Legion of Mary

The Legion of Mary is a lay apostolic association of Catholics who, with the sanction of the Church and under the powerful leadership of Mary Immaculate, serve the Church and their neighbor on a voluntary basis in about 170 countries. The group meets weekly for prayer, planning, and discussion in a family setting, and performs apostolic work in the parish community.

Bible Cell

The Bible Cell is dedicated to the study and sharing of the Word of God. Members meet regularly to reflect on Scripture, deepen their understanding of the faith, and encourage one another to live out the Gospel in daily life.

Small Christian Communities (SCC)

Our parish is divided into 24 Small Christian Communities (SCCs), each fostering faith, fellowship, and service at the neighborhood level. SCCs meet regularly for prayer, Bible sharing, and to support one another in living out the Christian faith. They play a vital role in building a sense of belonging and active participation in parish life.
