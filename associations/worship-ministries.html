Worship Ministries

Music Ministry

The Music Ministry animates the liturgy by leading the congregation in song and providing instrumental accompaniment. The ministry includes choir members, musicians, and cantors who serve at Sunday Masses, feast days, and special parish events. Their goal is to foster active participation and a prayerful atmosphere through music.

Liturgy Ministry

The Liturgy Ministry works closely with the priest and other parish groups to plan and execute all liturgical celebrations. This includes preparing the church, organizing processions, and ensuring that all aspects of the liturgy are carried out with devotion and care. The ministry helps make each celebration meaningful for the parish community.
