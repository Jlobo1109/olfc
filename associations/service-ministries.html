Service Ministries

Altar Servers

The Altar Servers are a dedicated group of young parishioners who assist the priest during Mass and other liturgical services. They are trained in the rituals and responsibilities of the altar, helping to ensure that the liturgy is conducted with reverence and order. The group meets regularly for practice and spiritual formation.

Liturgy Team

The Liturgy Team is responsible for the preparation and smooth conduct of all liturgical celebrations in the parish. This includes organizing lectors, extraordinary ministers of Holy Communion, altar servers, and coordinating with the choir. The team ensures that every Mass and service is prayerful and meaningful for the congregation.

Choir

The Choir leads the congregation in singing hymns and responses during Mass and special occasions. Members of the choir practice regularly and are committed to enhancing the liturgical experience through music. The choir also participates in parish events, feast days, and other celebrations.
