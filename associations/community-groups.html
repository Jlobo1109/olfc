Community Groups

Ladies Sodality

The Ladies Sodality is a group of women parishioners dedicated to fostering devotion to Our Lady, supporting parish activities, and engaging in charitable works. Members meet regularly for prayer, spiritual formation, and to organize events that benefit the parish and the wider community.

Senior Citizens

The Senior Citizens Group provides fellowship, support, and activities for the elderly members of the parish. The group organizes social gatherings, spiritual enrichment, and outreach to homebound seniors, helping them stay connected to parish life.
